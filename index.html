Spendly

A beautiful, private, and ad-free personal finance tracker. Built natively for Android using Kotlin & Jetpack Compose.

Features

Multi-Currency

Track expenses in 50+ currencies with automatic exchange rate updates.

Privacy First

100% ad-free with zero user tracking. Your data stays on your device.

Cloud Backups

Secure, encrypted backups directly to your private Google Drive.

Excel Export

Easy import and export functionality for managing data in your favorite spreadsheet tool.
